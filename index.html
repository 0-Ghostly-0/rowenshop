How payment works

No payment is required to send this request — I'll review it and follow up with a quote, estimated completion date, and deposit amount. Once you accept, a deposit books your spot in the queue, you'll see a watermarked preview before anything final, and the remaining balance is due before unwatermarked files are sent.

Prefer not to wait? You're welcome to pay now instead — in full or as a deposit, whatever amount you're comfortable with — using any option below.

CashApp Venmo Apple Pay Pay with Card

Paying now is completely optional — CashApp, Venmo, and card via Stripe all work whether you're covering a deposit or the full project cost upfront, and the Stripe checkout lets you type in whatever amount that is. Whichever way you pay, include your project name or Discord username (Stripe has a field for this) so I can match it to your request. Apple Pay isn't set up for an auto-link — DM me on Discord for that one instead.
